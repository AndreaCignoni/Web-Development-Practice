Who We Are

Welcome to the Retro Games Club – a student-led society for anyone who loves the classics. From boardgames and early console gems to old-school PC titles and childhood playground games, we bring together MTU students who share a passion for retro fun, nostalgia, and community.

About This Website

This website serves as the central hub for everything our society offers. Here you can browse upcoming events, explore retro gaming activities, register for tournaments, and sign up to become a member.

Benefits of Joining the Club

  • Access to exclusive retro gaming events and themed nights.
  • Opportunity to compete in tournaments and win prizes.
  • Meet new friends who share your gaming interests.
  • Try rare or vintage games not easily available today.
  • Get involved in running events and shaping the society.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Do I need gaming experience to join?

No experience is required! Our events are beginner-friendly and open to everyone.

2. Is there a membership fee?

No. The Retro Games Club is free for all MTU students.

3. How often do events take place?

We host major events each semester and smaller meetups throughout the year.

4. Can I bring my own games or consoles?

Absolutely! Students often bring personal favourites for others to try.
