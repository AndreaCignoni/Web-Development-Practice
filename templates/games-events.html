Upcoming club events — dates, locations and how to join
Event Name Date & Time Location How to join
Boardgame Bonanza Night February 20, 2026 · 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM Rory Gallagher Theatre Free for MTU students. Sign up via the Retro Games Club mailing list or at the student union desk.
A social evening dedicated to classics like Chess, Risk, and Cluedo. Students can bring their own boardgames or join tables hosted by the club.

Hurry up or you will regret it. Be there or be squared!
Arcade Revival Party March 28, 2026 · 5:00 PM - 10:00 PM MTU Sports Hall Register online through the MTU Sports Hall booking system. Entry is free, but spots are limited.
Relive the golden age of arcade machines with Pac-Man, Space Invaders, and Street Fighter II setups. Includes a “high score challenge” leaderboard.
Retro Console Night May 16, 2026 · 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM Rory Gallagher Theatre Sign up at the Retro Games Club website. Bring your own controller if you want to compete.
A throwback to consoles like the NES, Sega Mega Drive, and PlayStation 1. Multiplayer Mario Kart and Tekken tournaments will run throughout the night.
Playground Games Festival September 12, 2026 · 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM MTU Arena No registration needed. Just show up in sports gear and join a team on the day.
Outdoor fun with schoolyard classics like Red Rover, Bulldog, and Hopscotch. A lighthearted event to mix nostalgia with fitness.
